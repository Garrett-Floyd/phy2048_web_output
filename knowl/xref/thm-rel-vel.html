Theorem 8.3.2.

Let \(\vec{v^{\, \prime}}\) be the velocity of observer \(B\) in observer \(A\)’s reference frame. Let \(\vec{V}\) be the velocity of the object in observer \(B\)’s reference frame. Let \(\vec{v}\) be the velocity of the object in observer \(A\)’s reference frame. Then
\begin{equation*} \vec{V} = \vec{v} - \vec{v}^{\, \prime}. \end{equation*}
🔗
🔗

Proof.

From Figure 8.2.1 we see that
\begin{equation*} \vec{r}^{\, \prime} + \vec{R} = \vec{r}. \end{equation*}
Then
\begin{align*} \frac{d}{dt}(\vec{r}^{\, \prime} + \vec{R}) \amp = \frac{d\vec{r}}{dt} \\ \vec{v}^{\, \prime} + \vec{V} \amp = \vec{v} \\ \vec{V} \amp = \vec{v} - \vec{v}^{\, \prime} \end{align*}
