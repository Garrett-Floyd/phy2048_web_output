Theorem 15.2.1. Work-Kinetic Energy Theorem.

The net work done on a system is given by
\begin{equation*} W_{net} = \Delta K \end{equation*}
🔗
🔗

Proof.

\begin{align*} W_{net} \amp = \int_{C} \vec{F}_{net} \cdot d\vec{l} \\ \\ \amp = \int_{t_{i}}^{t_{f}} \vec{F}_{net} \cdot \vec{v} dt \\ \\ \amp = \int_{t_{i}}^{t_{f}} m \left( \vec{a} \cdot \vec{v} \right) dt \\ \\ \amp = \int_{t_{i}}^{t_{f}} m \left( \frac{d\vec{v}}{dt} \cdot \vec{v} \right) dt \\ \\ \amp = \int_{t_{i}}^{t_{f}} m \left( \frac{dv_{x}}{dt} v_{x} + \frac{dv_{y}}{dt} v_{y} + \frac{dv_{z}}{dt}v_{z} \right) dt \\ \\ \amp = \int_{t_{i}}^{t_{f}} m v_{x} \frac{dv_{x}}{dt} dt + \int_{t_{i}}^{t_{f}} m v_{y} \frac{dv_{y}}{dt} dt + \int_{t_{i}}^{t_{f}} m v_{z} \frac{dv_{z}}{dt} dt\\ \\ \amp = \int_{v_{x, i}}^{v_{x, f}} m v_{x} dv_{x} + \int_{v_{y, i}}^{v_{y, f}} m v_{y} dv_{y} + \int_{v_{z, i}}^{v_{z, f}} m v_{z} dv_{z}\\ \\ \amp = \left. \left[ \frac{1}{2} m v_{x}^{2} \right] \right|_{v_{x, i}}^{v_{x, f}} + \left. \left[ \frac{1}{2} m v_{y}^{2} \right] \right|_{v_{y, i}}^{v_{y, f}} + \left. \left[ \frac{1}{2} m v_{z}^{2} \right] \right|_{v_{z, i}}^{v_{z, f}} \\ \\ \amp = \frac{1}{2} m \left( v_{x, f}^{2} + v_{y, f}^{2} + v_{z, f}^{2} \right) - \frac{1}{2} m \left( v_{x, i}^{2} + v_{y, i}^{2} + v_{z, i}^{2} \right) \\ \\ \amp = \frac{1}{2} m v_{f}^{2} - \frac{1}{2} m v_{i}^{2} \\ \\ \amp = K_{f} - K_{i} \\ \\ \amp = \Delta K \end{align*}
